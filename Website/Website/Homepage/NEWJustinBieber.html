Purpose

Purpose is the fourth studio album by Canadian singer and songwriter Justin Bieber. While promoting his third studio album, Believe, Bieber confirmed in January 2013 that he was writing for a new album. Purpose was considered as an album with songs implying a 'regretful look back and a fresh start'. The themes of this album were seen as an 'apology' to Bieber's ex-girlfreind, Selena Gomez as well to his public for various misdemeanours. After working on this album for over two years, Bieber was having a tough time trying to find a musical direction for recording. But with the help of his friend and collaborator, Jason Boyd, Bieber started writing and recording with the thought of creating a greatly inspirational album as an apology of all his media scrutiny and his involvment in various misdemeanours.
